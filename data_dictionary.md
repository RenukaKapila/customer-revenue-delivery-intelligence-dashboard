# Data Dictionary

## orders
Contains order-level information, including order status, purchase date, approval date, estimated delivery date, and actual delivery date.

Important columns:
- order_id
- customer_id
- order_status
- order_purchase_timestamp
- order_delivered_customer_date
- order_estimated_delivery_date

## reviews
Contains customer review information.

Important columns:
- review_id
- order_id
- review_score
- review_comment_message
